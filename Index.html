About Trident Hills

Trident Hills is a thoughtfully planned premium residential township in Panchkula, offering spacious low-rise luxury floors surrounded by hills and forest views. Designed for families who value privacy, open spaces, and long-term lifestyle appreciation, the project combines nature-driven living with modern urban connectivity.

Developed by Trident Realty, the township emphasizes low-density planning, wide internal roads, landscaped greens, and high construction standards — making it suitable for both end-users and long-term investors.
